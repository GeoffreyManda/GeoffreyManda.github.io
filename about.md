      <div class="expertise-area">

      </div>
      <div class="expertise-area">
        <h3>Healthcare Analytics</h3>
        <p>Statistical approaches to understanding clinical outcomes, including predictive modeling, risk stratification, and treatment effectiveness evaluation in real-world clinical settings.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="expertise-area">
        <h3>Epidemiological Methods</h3>
        <p>Study design and analysis for public health research, with expertise in causal inference, mixed-effects modeling, and hierarchical data structures in medical research.</p>
      </div>
